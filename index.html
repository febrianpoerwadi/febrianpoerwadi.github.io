About Me

My name is Febrian Dwinanda Poerwadi, an event photographer who began his career while still in high school in 2023. He is currently studying at the Bandung Ministry of Health Polytechnic, majoring in D4 Health Promotion, where they excel in media. I have experience and interest in photography, specializing in documentary and event photography. Over the past two years, I have helped document various events, including school events, music festivals, graduations, and more.

I hope to continue creating memorable work at your events in the future!
